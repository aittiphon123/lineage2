Lineage 2 - Wolf Waker
https://l2wiki.com/main/updates/3222.html

## New Class and Skills
- Addition of a new class called Warg, which is exclusive to male Humans and can only be chosen as a main class.
- The Warg class has unique skills and abilities, including the ability to transform into a Werewolf during battle and can activate physical and hidden instincts to change its fighting style.
- New skills have also been added for existing classes, including Othell Rogue and Feoh Wizard, which require Enhanced Rune Stones to be learned.
- Tyrr Warrior's new buff skills, Savage Cry and Wild Cry, have been added, replacing old skills like Feral Bear Cry and Hawk Cry.

## Changes to Existing Classes and Skills
- Yul Archer skills have been modified, including changes to Sagittarius' Shooting Star, Sniping and Arrow Rain of Abyss.
- Wynn Summoner has new skills, including Summon Cat Sage, Summon Unicorn Mikhail and Summon Demoness, available from level 120 with Enhanced Rune Stones.
- Perfection now applies to basic attacks, physical and magic skills, with changes to its trigger rate and power and added effects to equipment of L-grade and higher.
- A new skill, Forgotten Power - Sea of Stars, is available to level 115+ characters, increasing max HP/MP and Soulshot/Spiritshot damage.

## New Features and Game Mechanics
- The Plunderous Plains are now a party hunting zone with invasion times at 12:00-13:00 and 19:00-20:00, where killing enough monsters can spawn Chaos Golems' War Machine for a special reward.
- A new field raid against Mighty Chaos Golem has been added, spawning at 13:00, 16:00 and 20:00 in the Plunderous Plains, with rewards based on damage dealt.
- The Spirit Forest party instance zone has a new difficulty level for level 125 characters, with a daily reset at 6:30 a.m.
- Silvery Circlet and Silvery Bracelet can now be enchanted using Gran Kain's Scroll, with various effects and success rates for each enchantment level.

## New Items and Equipment
- The Silvery Seed Bracelet can be enchanted using Gran Kain's Scroll: Enchant Silvery Accessories, with effects including increased attack power, debuff resistance and P./M. Def.
- Three new L-grade accessories have been added: Dark Dragon's Necklace, Earring and Ring, which can be obtained by upgrading +10 Dragon's accessories.
- Dragon's, Angel's and Fallen Angel's accessories can now be exchanged for corresponding 3rd level accessories with a 100% chance, using materials such as Selenite and Giant's Power.
- Some effects of Dragon's and Angel's accessories have been improved, including increased basic attack power when enchanted.
- The Light Dragon's Cloak can now be enchanted up to +10, with various effects such as AoE Damage Resistance and All Weapon Resistance.
- A new item called Dark Dragon's Radiant Cloak has been added, with base enchantment effects including All stats +5 and Received damage -20%.
- Additional enchant values have been added for attack artifacts, including Artifact - Basic Attack Power.
- New dyes have been added, including Legendary Dye (Basic Attack Power) and Eraton's Dye (Basic Attack Power), which increase Basic Attack Power.

## Changes to Ability System and Collections
- The ability system has been changed, with all ability points reset and some abilities replaced or improved, such as Berserker's Chaos Attack and Guardian's Evasion.
- New collections have been added, including Kasha Orc Victory Certificate - Ancient War Spirits and Plunderous Plains - Aerial War Machine Remains, with various effects such as increased accuracy and damage.
- Plunderous Plains collections provide various bonuses such as increased damage, XP/SP and accuracy, with required items like Dream War Machine Worker's Belt and Distorted Dream Fragment.
- Purple Orbs can replace certain items in collections like Weapon Enchantment II and Armor Enchantment I-III.

## Other Updates and Changes
- Many fishing items will be deleted after the update on August 20th, 2025 and replaced with Ancient Fisherman's Coins, which can be exchanged for items like Heine's Hourglass and Heine's Special Potion.
- Tales of Hero - Travels has been added, with 100 additional levels, providing effects like increased attribute attack and resistance and acquired XP/SP, with rewards including Sealed Rune of the Unknown One and Tales of Hero - Book of Knowledge upgrades.
- New items have been added to Einhasad's Store, including Purple Orb, Spellbook: Create Mysterious Spiritshots and Spellbook: Create Mysterious Soulshots.
- The limit of certain items sold for adena has been changed, such as Aether, Tales of Dimensions and Dye Powder.
- New crafting items have been added, including Ancient Dwarves' Forgotten Spellbook chapters and Scroll: Enchant Sign of Common Dream.
- Various issues and bugs have been fixed, including problems with character transformation, raid points and quest rewards.
- Changes have been made to the game's mechanics, such as the reflection of M. damage and the display of adena for sale through the World Trade.
- Some items and features have been removed or modified, including the raid points system, certain exchange options and the Superior Seed Bracelet Lv. 4 enchantment option.


Lineage 2 - Orc Village

## Skill Updates
- The update includes changes to skills for various classes, including Aeore Healer, Aeore Eva's Saint, Aeore Cardinal and Aeore Shillien Saint.
- New skills have been added, such as Eva's Recharge, Shield Light and Expulsion, while existing skills like Radiant Heal, Panic Heal and Summon Tree of Life have been modified.
- Changes have been made to debuff resistance skills, stat increase buff skills and protective skills, including Brilliant Purge, Radiant Purge, Solid Resistance and Cardinal's Divine Barrier.
- Shillien's Divine Barrier now has a 15-second duration, 100,000 HP and consumes 10 Spirit Ore, replacing other barrier effects.
- Debuff skills such as Reset, Erase Impact, Dark Devour, Dark Backfire and Fatal Sleep have been updated with increased ranges and effects.
- New skills have been added for Tyrr Warrior and Yul Archer classes, including Omen of Doom, Storm's Eye and Arrow Rain of Abyss, which require Enhanced Rune Stones to learn.
- Class balance adjustments have been made, including improved skill power calculations for Tyrr Warrior, Yul Archer and Eviscerator and changes to skills such as Master's Touch, Titan's Strike and Multiple Moonlit Arrows.

## New Hunting Zones and Field Bosses
- The game has introduced new hunting zones near the Orc Village, including Immortal Plateau, Kasha Orcs' Logging Site and Kasha Orc Fortress, with increased monster stats and debuffs.
- A new Field Boss, Atrus, has been added to the game, appearing in the Kasha Orc Fortress every Tuesday and Thursday at 21:00, with rewards for defeating him.
- The rewards for Valakas have been changed, with new items added and some removed, including Supreme Krishna Weapon and Supreme Leviathan Armor.
- A new seasonal solo instance zone, Abandoned Cave of Trials, has been added, with rewards based on completion time, including temporary buffs like Tenacity Reward Lv. 1-5.

## Instance Zones and Rewards
- A new seasonal solo instance zone, Abandoned Cave of Trials, has been added, with rewards based on completion time, including temporary buffs like Tenacity Reward Lv. 1-5.
- Tenacity Rewards have been added for completing the weekly instance zone 'Forsaken Cave of Trials', with 5 levels of rewards providing increased accuracy, resistance and experience gain.
- A new hunting zone, 'Isle of Prayer', has been added to the Ravaged Innadril session zone, available to characters level 125 and above.

## Conquest World and Hunting Zone Changes
- Changes have been made to the Conquest world, including spawn times and rewards, as well as fixes to issues with the hunting zone display and reward payouts.
- Various hunting zones have been modified, with some low-level field raids and instance zones removed and the accuracy of certain NPCs increased.

## Item Updates
- New items have been added, including the Silvery Shirt enchantment and Blood Rune Tainted by Chaos, with associated crafting fees and materials.
- The effects of Rune of Chaos Tainted Blood have been detailed, with levels 3-10 providing various buffs such as increased XP/SP, damage and resistance when fighting Kasha Orcs.
- A new Chaos Augmenting Stone: Weapon/Armor has been introduced, allowing augmentation of certain weapons and armor with various properties.
- Several item changes have been made, including changes to crystallization, augmentation and removal prices, as well as updates to item descriptions and effects.

## Quest and System Changes
- New main quests have been added, starting a story about the Power of Chaos, with solo instance zones and rewards for completion.
- System changes have been made, including updates to the chat window, such as blocking chat affecting world chat and improved display of content.
- New items have been added to Einhasad's Store, including the Mysterious Soul Sphere Lv. 1 and Dragon Skin Shirt.

## Miscellaneous Updates
- Changes have been made to the Wyvern, including new dismounting conditions and a changed summoning cost from B-grade Crystal x25 to Crystal x100.
- Clan War declaration conditions have been modified, allowing a Lv. 5+ clan to declare war regardless of the number of clan members.
- The Accuracy and Evasion formula has been modified, with P. Accuracy now affecting P. skill success rate and a level-based Accuracy bonus has been added.
- New collections have been added, including the Kasha Orcs collections, which provide various effects such as increased damage and defense.

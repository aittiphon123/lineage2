## Skill Updates
- The update is scheduled for release on April 23, 2025, and includes changes to skills for various classes, including Aeore Healer, Aeore Eva's Saint, Aeore Cardinal, and Aeore Shillien Saint.
- New skills have been added, such as Eva's Recharge, Shield Light, and Expulsion, while existing skills like Radiant Heal, Panic Heal, and Summon Tree of Life have been modified.
- Changes have been made to debuff resistance skills, stat increase buff skills, and protective skills, including Brilliant Purge, Radiant Purge, Solid Resistance, and Cardinal's Divine Barrier.
- Shillien's Divine Barrier now has a 15-second duration, 100,000 HP, and consumes 10 Spirit Ore, replacing other barrier effects.
- Debuff skills such as Reset, Erase Impact, Dark Devour, Dark Backfire, and Fatal Sleep have been updated with increased ranges and effects.
- New skills have been added for Tyrr Warrior and Yul Archer classes, including Omen of Doom, Storm's Eye, and Arrow Rain of Abyss, which require Enhanced Rune Stones to learn.
- Class balance adjustments have been made, including improved skill power calculations for Tyrr Warrior, Yul Archer, and Eviscerator, and changes to skills such as Master's Touch, Titan's Strike, and Multiple Moonlit Arrows.

## New Hunting Zones and Field Bosses
- The game has introduced new hunting zones near the Orc Village, including Immortal Plateau, Kasha Orcs' Logging Site, and Kasha Orc Fortress, with increased monster stats and debuffs.
- A new Field Boss, Atrus, has been added to the game, appearing in the Kasha Orc Fortress every Tuesday and Thursday at 21:00, with rewards for defeating him.
- The rewards for Valakas have been changed, with new items added and some removed, including Supreme Krishna Weapon and Supreme Leviathan Armor.
- A new seasonal solo instance zone, Abandoned Cave of Trials, has been added, with rewards based on completion time, including temporary buffs like Tenacity Reward Lv. 1-5.

## Instance Zones and Rewards
- A new seasonal solo instance zone, Abandoned Cave of Trials, has been added, with rewards based on completion time, including temporary buffs like Tenacity Reward Lv. 1-5.
- Tenacity Rewards have been added for completing the weekly instance zone 'Forsaken Cave of Trials', with 5 levels of rewards providing increased accuracy, resistance, and experience gain.
- A new hunting zone, 'Isle of Prayer', has been added to the Ravaged Innadril session zone, available to characters level 125 and above.

## Conquest World and Hunting Zone Changes
- Changes have been made to the Conquest world, including spawn times and rewards, as well as fixes to issues with the hunting zone display and reward payouts.
- Various hunting zones have been modified, with some low-level field raids and instance zones removed, and the accuracy of certain NPCs increased.

## Item Updates
- New items have been added, including the Silvery Shirt enchantment and Blood Rune Tainted by Chaos, with associated crafting fees and materials.
- The effects of Rune of Chaos Tainted Blood have been detailed, with levels 3-10 providing various buffs such as increased XP/SP, damage, and resistance when fighting Kasha Orcs.
- A new Chaos Augmenting Stone: Weapon/Armor has been introduced, allowing augmentation of certain weapons and armor with various properties.
- Several item changes have been made, including changes to crystallization, augmentation, and removal prices, as well as updates to item descriptions and effects.

## Quest and System Changes
- New main quests have been added, starting a story about the Power of Chaos, with solo instance zones and rewards for completion.
- System changes have been made, including updates to the chat window, such as blocking chat affecting world chat and improved display of content.
- New items have been added to Einhasad's Store, including the Mysterious Soul Sphere Lv. 1 and Dragon Skin Shirt.

## Miscellaneous Updates
- Changes have been made to the Wyvern, including new dismounting conditions and a changed summoning cost from B-grade Crystal x25 to Crystal x100.
- Clan War declaration conditions have been modified, allowing a Lv. 5+ clan to declare war regardless of the number of clan members.
- The Accuracy and Evasion formula has been modified, with P. Accuracy now affecting P. skill success rate, and a level-based Accuracy bonus has been added.
- New collections have been added, including the Kasha Orcs collections, which provide various effects such as increased damage and defense.

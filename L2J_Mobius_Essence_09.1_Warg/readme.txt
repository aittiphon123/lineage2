## Warg Class Update
- The Warg is a new hunting class that can only be played by male Humans, and they can only fight with Fist Weapons and use sigils, but not shields.
- Wargs have a unique appearance that changes depending on their upper armor grade and when they wear a Heavenly Cloak, and they gain a distinctive transformation depending on their ranking.
- After the 2nd class change, Wargs receive special points (WP) and can transform into a wolf, gaining access to unique buffs and skills, such as Moon's Grace, Double Claw Strike, and Claw Sweep.
- The Warg has skills such as Direct Strike, Upward Strike, Devastating Assault, and Quick Dash, which change into different skills like Double Claw Strike and Claw Sweep when transformed into a wolf.
- The Warg's transformation into a wolf recovers MP, decreases received damage, increases Speed, and increases Fear Atk. Rate and Resistance.
- The Warg has access to various skills and enhancements, including Growing Potential, Supreme Agility, Breaking Instinct, and Ancient Power, which provide different benefits such as increased P. Atk., P. Def., and M. Def.
- The Cardinal class has been changed, with enhanced healing abilities and the addition of the Dark Side skill, which allows the class to perform an attacking role.

## Cardinal Class Update
- The Cardinal class has been changed, with enhanced healing abilities and the addition of the Dark Side skill, which allows the class to perform an attacking role.
- The Dark Side skill turns healing skills into attack skills and applies debuffs to enemies, and its master version, Темная Сторона: Мастер, summons an Abyssal Spear and enhances attack skills.
- The document 'Warg — Knowledge base Lineage II Essence' describes various skills, including Atk. -50%- Mana Parasite, Shining Beam, Abyss Beam, and others, with their effects and enhancements.

## Skill Updates
- The document 'Warg — Knowledge base Lineage II Essence' describes various skills, including Atk. -50%- Mana Parasite, Shining Beam, Abyss Beam, and others, with their effects and enhancements.
- Skills are categorized by rarity, with descriptions such as Common, Enhanced, Superior, Rare, Heroic, and Legendary, replacing the previous star rating system.
- Several skills have been modified, including Restore Life, Unleashing, Shining Wave, and Divine Recovery, with changes to their effects, casting times, and consumption of Spirit Ore.
- New skills and effects have been added, such as Shining Chain Heal, Chain Explosion, and Divine Whisper, while some skills have been removed or replaced, like Heal and Battle Heal being replaced by Greater Heal and Greater Battle Heal.
- New attacking skills have been added for certain classes, including Transcendent Relentless Attack for Assassin and Transcendent Blade Throw for Duelist.
- The cost of acquiring certain skills has been changed, with some skills now requiring Magic Tablets instead of adena.
- The effects of the Blessed by Sayha skill have been changed to increase Max HP and PvE damage.

## Jewel and Item Updates
- The former 8 Jewels have been replaced by 4 new Jewels: Ruby, Emerald, Sapphire, and Aquamarine, each with unique effects and levels.
- New jewels can be compounded to create higher-level jewels, with varying compounding success rates.
- The items obtained from the Selected Jewel Coupon [Sealed] have been updated with new quantities and coupon requirements for various Jewelry Box levels.
- A new Special Armor, Sea Boots, has been added with enchant value effects on stats such as STR, INT, CON, MEN, P. Def, M. Def, and Max HP.
- The effects of several items have been improved, including Core's Ring, Antharas' Earring, and Talisman of Baium, with changes to fear resistance, attack power, and enchanting probabilities.
- The Cloak of Protection can now be blessed with the Scroll of Blessing - Special, applying appearance and blessing effects with certain probabilities.
- Soulshot/Spiritshot consumption has been changed based on the number of targets for basic and skill attacks.
- The Talisman of Hellbound can now be obtained through a guaranteed exchange using Hellbound Energy [Sealed] coupons.
- New effects have been added to the Weapons of Immortality and the Death Knight's Flame Sword.
- The Weapons of Immortality and Death Knight's Flame Sword have a buff that can be applied with a certain probability, increasing PvE damage and acquired XP.

## Hunting Zone Updates
- A new hunting zone, Wolfmen Camp, has been added to the Warg Settlement area with various monsters and rewards.
- The probability of the Shifting Mirage monster invasion has been increased, and the rewards for killing certain bosses have been improved.
- The HP of bosses in world special hunting zones has been increased, and the rewards from Ferion's Treasure Chest [Sealed] have been improved.
- The Frintezza Raid rewards have been changed, and the loot distribution method has been changed to "Raid Auction".
- Hunting zone changes include reduced Adena rewards in certain zones on Essence and Eva servers, and increased XP gains in other zones.
- A new item, Doll Summon Coupon - 1-time [Sealed], can be obtained with a certain chance when killing common monsters on Eva server.
- The World Tower of Insolence has reduced P. Atk. for certain monsters and decreased acquired XP/SP in Lost Canyon.
- New world special hunting zones have been added, including Ironheart Fortress - Supply Depot and Ironheart Fortress, with entry levels of Lv. 90+ and various rewards.
- Fafurion's Nest world raid dungeon is now available on Sundays at 19:00 on the Eva server, with a party search and increasing difficulty levels as the boss Fafurion progresses through three phases.

## Raid and Dungeon Updates
- Fafurion's Nest world raid dungeon is now available on Sundays at 19:00 on the Eva server, with a party search and increasing difficulty levels as the boss Fafurion progresses through three phases.
- Defeating Fafurion's minions rewards each raid member with Fafurion's Treasure Chest [Sealed], while defeating Fafurion rewards each member with Fafurion's Glittering Treasure Chest [Sealed] and the final blow dealer with Lucky Pack: 2 Billion [Sealed].
- Fafurion's Treasure Chest [Sealed] and Fafurion's Glittering Treasure Chest [Sealed] contain various items, including Dye Booster [Sealed], Augmenting Stones, and Spellbook Coupons.
- The Cyrax's Temple [Eva] raid has a daily entry time from 21:00 to 21:05, requires a party of 5 level 88+ characters, and rewards participants with items such as High Priest Cyrax's Treasure Chest [Sealed] and Dragon's Minion Pack [Sealed].
- The Water Dragon's Canyon has been expanded and is available for hunting on Saturdays and Sundays for players level 85+ on the Eva server.

- Changes have been made to the Dream Dungeon, including new entry requirements, loot, and rewards, and it is now bound to account with a Dream Orb reward available from a chest.

- New missions have been added, including "Explore Ironheart Fortress", "Hunt in the Ironheart Fortress", "Fight with the Legion of Darkness", "Assault on Cyrax's Temple", and "Assault on the Iron Supply Depot", with rewards like Hidden Power Booster and Dragon's Minion Pack.

## Gameplay and Auction System Updates
- The mail service fee has been changed, with new prices for sending regular and paid mail.
- A raid auction system has been introduced, allowing players to bid on valuable trophies from raid bosses, with the highest contributor having the right to pick up the reward.
- The auction system allows characters to bid on items, with the winner being the one with the highest bid after 15 seconds, and the amount being distributed among party members after tax deduction.
- The class change process has been improved, allowing characters to change class through a special interface, with the option to retrieve skills using Aden's Blessing [Sealed] items.
- Various updates have been made to the game, including changes to the Grocers, Black Coupons, Game Assistant, chat system, free teleports, and entrance to special zones, as well as fixes to several bugs and improvements to the overall gameplay experience.

## Bug Fixes and Other Updates
- Various updates have been made to the game, including changes to the Grocers, Black Coupons, Game Assistant, chat system, free teleports, and entrance to special zones, as well as fixes to several bugs and improvements to the overall gameplay experience.
- The Warg update fixed various bugs, including issues with gear repair, skill resets, and item descriptions.
- The update also improved the Aden Essence description and fixed bugs related to enchanting items, such as the Blessed Piercing Mask, Blessed Phantom Mask, and Blessed Talisman of Authority.

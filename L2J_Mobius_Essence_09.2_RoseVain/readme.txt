## Blood Rose Hunter Class
- The Blood Rose Hunter class has been added, exclusive to Dark Elves, using only a dagger in battle and having a unique appearance that changes with upper armor grade.- Blood Rose has access to various battle skills, including Rose Attack, Bleeding Rose Attack, Vine Embrace and others, with effects varying by distance to the target and skill level.
- The class also has unique buffs, such as Enchanted Rose's Magic, Crimson Rose and Kingdom of Plants, which enhance magic abilities, attack power and critical rates, among other benefits.
- Blood Rose has various skills, including Reflecting Illusion, which recovers 50% of Max HP and provides other defensive benefits and skills like Ghost Step, Eternal Bloom and Enchanted Dagger Mastery.

## Shillien and Eva's Saints
- Shillien Saint has new and improved skills, including Shillien's Grail, which strengthens the character with the power of the impure Shillien's chalice and improved skills like Nemesis and Enchanted Dark Beam.- Eva's Saint has a new skill, Eva's Grail, which is similar to Shillien's Grail, providing a boost to M. Atk., M. Skill Power and PvE damage.
- Eva's Nocturne skill has been improved with decreased cooldown and new effects.
- Several skills have been improved, including Enchanted Sea Beam, Prophecy of Water, Enchanted Sea Oath, Cure and Weight Loss.

## Skill Changes and Additions
- New skills have been added, such as Vortex of Fire, Crimson Flame, Shining Prison and Flame Heart.- Some skills have been deleted, including Eagle Spirit, Soul Evasion and Spirit Shield.
- Changes have been made to existing skills, including Battle Cry, Life Rescue, Freezing Flame, Chant of Vampire and Prophecy of Victory.
- Multiple skills have been changed or improved, including Team Building, Blood Bond, Chant of Life, Seal of Disease, Seal of Infection and Prophecy of Pa'agrio.
- New skills have been added, such as the Flame Heart passive skill and Legendary Skills for Rogue classes, including Veil of Darkness: Master, Veil of Wind: Master and Veil of Phantom: Master.
- Some skills have been deleted, including Seal of Flame, Seal of Gloom, Pa'agrio's Heart and Chant of Life.
- The legendary skills for the Rogue character archetype have been updated, increasing Vital Point Attack power and adding new effects.
- The effects of various skills, including Backstab, Shadow Step and Flying Dagger, have been changed, with updates to cooldowns, skill ranges and damage output.
- The Rogue Agility skill has been updated, granting immunity to standard de-stealthing skills when DEX is 75 or higher and increasing physical skill power.
- Several skills, including Critical Assault, Fury Blade and Mortal Piercing, have had their effects changed, with updates to damage output, critical rates and debuffs.
- Some skills, such as Sand Bomb, Bleed and Throw Dagger in Wrist, have been deleted.
- Changes have been made to various skills for classes such as Death Knight, Assassin, Elemental Master, Grand Khavatari, Titan, Eva's Templar, Shillien Templar, Divine Templar, Element Weaver, Archers and Storm Blaster, including changes to spirit ore consumption, skill power and effects.
- Some skills have been deleted, including Sand Bomb, Bleed, Throwing Blood Dagger, Hex and Trick.

## Dragon Weapons
- New Dragon Weapon types have been added, which can be obtained through the special craft menu and have triggered skills and effects depending on the weapon type.
- The max level of HP Recovery and MP Recovery skills has been increased, with new effects and spirit ore consumption added for each level.
- Dragon Weapons are A-grade and have various stats and effects, including triggered skills such as Aqua Gust, Fafurion's Stigma and Fafurion's Blessing.
- The stats and effects of Dragon Weapons increase with enchant value, with improvements to P. Atk, M. Atk, HP/MP/CP and skill trigger rates.
- There are four types of Dragon Weapons: Valakas, Lindvior, Fafurion and Antharas, each with unique skills and effects such as Flame Mark, Earth Breaker and Air Current.
- Dragon Weapons can be obtained from a Dragon Weapon Pack [Sealed] or by using a Purified Sphere of Immortality to change the type of an existing Dragon Weapon.
- The stats for Weapons of Immortality have been changed for each enchant value.

## Warg Class Update
- The Warg is a new hunting class that can only be played by male Humans and they can only fight with Fist Weapons and use sigils, but not shields.
- Wargs have a unique appearance that changes depending on their upper armor grade and when they wear a Heavenly Cloak and they gain a distinctive transformation depending on their ranking.
- After the 2nd class change, Wargs receive special points (WP) and can transform into a wolf, gaining access to unique buffs and skills, such as Moon's Grace, Double Claw Strike and Claw Sweep.
- The Warg has skills such as Direct Strike, Upward Strike, Devastating Assault and Quick Dash, which change into different skills like Double Claw Strike and Claw Sweep when transformed into a wolf.
- The Warg's transformation into a wolf recovers MP, decreases received damage, increases Speed and increases Fear Atk. Rate and Resistance.
- The Warg has access to various skills and enhancements, including Growing Potential, Supreme Agility, Breaking Instinct and Ancient Power, which provide different benefits such as increased P. Atk., P. Def. and M. Def.
- The Cardinal class has been changed, with enhanced healing abilities and the addition of the Dark Side skill, which allows the class to perform an attacking role.

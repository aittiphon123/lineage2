## Grand Khavatari Class Changes
- The Grand Khavatari class has received new skills: Cacophony of War: Master, Enhanced Hurricane, Jump Attack and Rushing Retreat.
- Some existing Grand Khavatari skills have been changed, including Cacophony of War, Armor Break (now Blazing Strike), Ogre's Essence, Master of Combat, Rabbit Spirit Totem, Puma Spirit Totem, Burning Assault, Bear Nature, Hawk Nature and Final Secret.
- The Grand Khavatari's Attack Mastery and Armor Mastery skills have replaced existing skills, including Fist Weapon Mastery, Weapon Mastery, Attack Speed Boost, Fury Fists, Stunning Fist, Iron Fist, Light Armor Mastery and Armor Mastery.

## Elemental Master Class Changes
- The Elemental Master class has received new skills: Elemental Discharge, Elemental Strike, Elemental Shackles, Elemental Vortex, Elemental Roots, Elemental Nurture and Elemental Concentration.
- The Elemental Master class has various skills, including Elemental Synchrony, Living Heart, Elemental Mastery and Unicorn's Training, which provide different effects and stat increases.
- The class also has summoning skills, such as Summon Elemental Unicorn, Summon Elemental Globe and Summon Elemental Cubic, which provide various benefits and combat effects.
- Skills of the Elemental Master class can be enchanted, with different enchant levels providing increased effects and stat boosts.

## Other Class Changes
- Other skill changes and improvements have been made to various classes, including Spectral Dancer, Doomcryer, Dominator, Eva's Templar and Adventurer.

## Hunting Zone Updates: Seal of Shillien
- The Seal of Shillien hunting zone has been updated with new monster spawns and chaotic bosses and players can obtain various rewards, including A-grade equipment and Aden's Exploration Report Pages.

## Raid Boss Updates
- New Spellbooks have been added to the raid boss trophy list, including Elemental Shackles, Elemental Mastery and Unicorn's Training.
- The list of Guardian Spellbooks that can be obtained for raids has been changed, with new spellbooks dropping from specific monsters.
- The spawn time of certain raid bosses has been changed, with some bosses disappearing if not defeated within 30 minutes.
- The HP of certain Raid Bosses, including Deiman, Satina, Ryuminir and Aizen Kelsour, has been increased.
- New rewards have been added for defeating certain raid bosses, including +5 ~ +7 Talisman of Aden Pack and Lucky Pack: 2 billion adena.
- Changes have been made to the spawn times and rewards of various raid bosses, including Antharas, Zaken and Queen Ant.

## Monster and Zone Reward Adjustments
- The adena amount dropping from monsters in certain locations has been changed and penalties are now applied to multiple targets on the 2nd and 3rd floors of the Kruma Tower.
- The amount of XP and SP received when killing monsters in Gorde Canyon has been increased and the Canyon Hunter now uses the Mass Knockback skill.
- Decreased rewards in Lost Nest and Hot Springs and deleted 5 Memory hunting zones.
- Added trophies and packages that can drop in certain hunting zones, including Venir's Talisman Fragment, Upgrade Stone Fragment, +5 ~ +7 Special Armor and +5 ~ +7 Boss Weapon.

## New and Updated Zones
- Introduced Fafurion's Underwater Canyon, a new world session zone with changing water levels and monsters and a daily mission to kill 100 monsters.
- United Transcendent instance zones into a special hunting zone available to characters Lv. 70+, with varying difficulty levels and rewards.
- Changed Celestial Tower requirements to Lv. 88, increased Celestial Emperor Ferion's health and added a new monster, the Heavenly Saint.
- Modified Aztacan's Temple settings, including reset time and rewards and changed the Raid Against Frintezza, including participants, duration and rewards.
- Introduced new features and rewards in the Raid Against Frintezza, including Vampiric Resistance, Halisha's Breath and increased monster power.
- The monster invasion zone has been changed for different servers and levels, with new zones including Dragon Valley, Inner Dragon Valley, Shifting Mirage, Melat Lizardman Barracks and Tayga Camp.

## Shifting Mirage Monster Invasion Zone
- Defeating intermediate bosses in the Shifting Mirage and lowering the final boss' HP to a certain level can reward players with Shifting Mirage's Treasure Chests containing various items.
- The Shifting Mirage's Treasure Chest contents and the Mirage Monster Hunt Reward Pack contents have been listed, including guaranteed and additional available items such as Aden's Exploration Report Pages, Spellbook Coupons and Skill Enchant Charging Stones.
- Common monsters now appear regularly in the Shifting Mirage monster invasion zone and players can teleport to the area using the Gludin hunting zones list or the recommended hunting zones tab.

## Legion of Darkness Invasion Update
- The reward for the Legion of Darkness Invasion has been changed, with new treasure chests containing various items such as Aden's Exploration Report Pages, Spellbook Coupons and Skill Enchant Charging Stones.

## Boss Extermination Event
- The "Battle with Balok" event has been renamed to "Boss Extermination" and takes place from Monday to Saturday, 20:30-21:30.
- The event has two modes: Regular and Special, with the Special mode featuring the Istina Boss.
- A Burial Beetle may appear after killing a boss, with a chance to drop a Burial Beetle Adena Pouch containing up to 100 million adena.

## Guardian System Updates
- New humanoid Guardians, Raven and Vulcan, have been added with unique skills and acquisition methods.
- The Guardian UI has been updated and the amount of equipment Guardians can carry has been increased from 12 to 17 pieces.
- The fee for studying Guardian Animal skills has been changed, with some skills now requiring Magic Tablets or Guardian Books.
- Guardian's Pendant and Einhasad's Pendant have been improved with increased stats and effects.
- Gran Kain's Pendant has been added with various effects and stats.
- Guardians can be sent by mail, but not if they have equipment on.
- Humanoid Guardians cannot use the extraction system, while Animal Guardians can.
- New Guardian Spellbooks and Guardian Books have been added, replacing Pet Books.
- Special P. Atk./ M. Atk. stats are applied when the Guardian receives certain items.
- A Guardian preview function has been added to learn information about a Guardian.

## Item Improvements and Additions
- Zaken's Earring and Einhasad's Pendant have been improved with increased stats and effects.
- Augmentation properties have been added to the Cloak of Protection, Aden Cloak and Heavenly Cloak.
- The stats of the Heavenly Cloak have been improved, with increased P. Atk., M. Atk., P. Def., M. Def., Max HP and Max MP.
- The properties of Blessed Piercing Mask and Blessed Phantom Mask have been added, with various enchant values and effects.
- The craft for Blessed Piercing Mask and Blessed Phantom Mask has been added, requiring specific items and adena.
- The stats of the Weapon of Immortality have been improved, with increased Max HP, MP and CP and additional effects on attack.
- The effects of Talisman Bracelet and Brooch have been improved, with increased P. Atk. and P. Def.

## System Changes and Additions
- System changes include a reduced cost of resurrection in clan wars and a new transcendent level in the Aden Laboratory.
- Transcendent Upgrade Stones and Greater Upgrade Stones can be used for upgrades, with 3 levels of transcendent upgrades offering base and special effects.
- A new Doll Summon system allows for direct summoning from the Summon tab, with added features such as success rate display and auto-summoning.
- new dolls have been added, including Princess Molrang Doll and Aztacan Doll, which can be obtained through summoning or compounding.
- Guaranteed compounding points can be earned and used to compound higher-grade dolls with a 100% success rate.
- new doll collections have been added, offering various properties and bonuses.

## L-Shop, Special Craft and Collection Updates
- New items have been added to the L-Shop, Special Craft and Collections, including Doll Summon Coupons, Purified Sphere of Immortality and Venir's Talisman Lv. 1.
- The Collection Coin (Time-limited) [Sealed] can be used as a replacement item in various collections, including Venir's Research, Hunter's Upper Armor Collection and Growth Rune Collection IV.

## Combat Power Value Adjustments
- Dolls, doll collections, Aden Laboratory, soul crystals and blessings now affect the combat power value.
- The combat power value of higher-grade dolls takes precedence and some doll collections also affect the combat power value.
- Aden Laboratory exploration, soul crystals and blessed equipment increase the combat power value.
- The combat power value of some items, such as Dragon Belt and Talisman of Aden, has been increased.

## Purge Reward Pack Changes
- The contents of 6 Purge Reward Packs have been changed.

## Pattern and Hidden Power System Updates
- The Patterns and Hidden Power systems have been separated and the effects of patterns can be the same if the same dye is applied.
- The Skill Critical Rate table has been updated with new values for Spiritshot damage, Critical Rate and other effects.
- The Hidden Power slot unlocking function has been added, allowing players to open closed slots using the breakthrough.
- The dye compounding fee has been changed, with new fees for different dye levels.

## Game Assistant and Store Updates
- The Game Assistant has been updated to allow purchasing supplies and stones for pets in the Service Store section.
- The availability of Spirit Ore has been changed and it can now be obtained from the L-Coin Store or Eva's Shop.
- New items have been added to the list of highest quality supplies sold by Grocers Holly and Helvetia.

## Blessing System Update
- The Blessing System has been updated, allowing players to apply blessing effects to sigils and shields using a Scroll of Blessing.

## Quest Updates and Bug Fixes
- Quest rewards have been changed for certain quests and bugs have been fixed in various quests.

## User Interface Updates
- The user interface has been updated, including changes to the inventory window, quest interface and other features.
- Recommended hunting zones have been added to the teleport list for easier access.

## Bug Fixes and Improvements
- Multiple bugs have been fixed, including issues with visual effects, skill descriptions and item operations.
- Various improvements have been made, including added notifications, improved descriptions and enhanced visual effects for certain items and events.

## High Elves and Class Updates
- High Elves have been added as a new playable race with two classes: Divine Templar (Knight) for male characters and Element Weaver (Wizard) for female characters.
- Divine Templar is a class that combines features of Warrior and Knight classes, using powerful magic of light and has unique skills, buffs and passive skills.
- Element Weaver is a class of High Elf wizards who master magic of fire, ice, nature and light, using two-handed blunt weapons and sigils.
- Divine Templar has a unique system called Light Points, which can be used to summon the Divine Guardian, a powerful ally that can aid in combat.
- High Elves have been added to the race ranking system, with the first-place High Elf receiving a buff that includes increased Skill Power, P. Atk., M. Atk., P. Def., M. Def., Atk. Spd., P. Accuracy and M. Accuracy.
- Element Weavers can use fire, ice and light magic, with skills such as Fire Sphere, Ice Sphere, Blazing Tempest, Glacier Strike and Extreme Magic.
- Light magic skills include Bright Dance, Claidheamh Soluis and Sephiroth, which can trigger additional effects like Mistilteinn and Light Influence.
- Nature magic skills, such as Nature Spirit, Florescence and One With Nature, provide buffs and debuffs and can be combined with light magic for enhanced effects.
- Element Weavers have passive skills, including Children of the Mother Tree, Serenade of Mana and Elemental Sphere Mastery, which enhance their magic abilities.

- Mystic Muse class has new skills, including Mystic Meteor: Master, Mystic Freeze, Winter Shackles, Winter Slumber and Winter Frostbite, which provide various effects such as damage, debuffs and crowd control.

- Fortune Seeker skills have been updated, including Fortune Time, Fortune Seeker's Blessing and Blessed Fortune Time, with enhanced effects and additional rewards.

- Fortune Time rewards for pet raids have been listed, including Pet's Pendant Lv. 1, Pet Book Coupons, Pet Earring, Pet Ring and Scroll: Enchant Pet Ornaments.
- Several class skills have been updated, including Titan Champion, Time Distortion: Master, Soul Hound's Resonance and Lightning Tornado.
- Class buffs have been categorized as passive skills and Spirit Ore consumption for some general class buffs has been changed.
